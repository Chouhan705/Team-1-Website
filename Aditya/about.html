About Chetak

Welcome to Chetak, your rapid emergency medical assistance navigator. In critical moments, finding the right hospital with the necessary facilities quickly can make all the difference. Chetak aims to bridge this gap by analyzing a patient's immediate condition and identifying the nearest suitable hospital equipped to handle the specific emergency.

Our Mission

To reduce delays in receiving critical medical care during emergencies by providing intelligent, location-aware hospital routing based on real-time needs and hospital capabilities.

How It Works

  1. Users input the patient's emergency condition (e.g., cardiac arrest, accident) or describe the symptoms via the simple interface.
  2. Chetak analyzes the input to determine the required medical resources (like ICU availability, specific specialists, or essential equipment).
  3. The system cross-references these needs with a database of nearby hospitals and their capabilities.
  4. It identifies the closest hospital that appropriately matches the patient's requirements based on facilities and distance.
  5. A map displaying the user's location and the optimal route to the recommended hospital is presented. Alternative suitable hospitals may also be shown.

This project is currently a demonstration developed for a college assignment. It utilizes static hospital data and a simplified condition analysis model to illustrate the core concept.
